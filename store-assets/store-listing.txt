APP NAME:
Screen Lock for Kids

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars):
Lock your screen while kids watch videos. Prevents accidental touches.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
Screen Lock for Kids helps parents prevent accidental touches while their children watch videos on Android devices.

FEATURES:
• Floating lock button - Stays on screen over any app (YouTube, WhatsApp, etc.)
• One-tap lock - Instantly block all touch input
• One-tap unlock - Tap the floating button again to restore touch
• Draggable - Move the floating button anywhere on screen
• Lightweight - No internet required, no data collection

HOW TO USE:
1. Open any video app (YouTube, Netflix, etc.)
2. Tap the floating lock button
3. Screen is locked - your child can watch without interruption
4. Tap the floating button again to unlock

PRIVACY:
• No internet access required
• No personal data collected
• No analytics or tracking
• Everything stays on your device

PERMISSIONS:
• Display over other apps - Required to show the floating lock button
• Foreground service - Keeps the floating button visible

NOTE: This app is designed for parental use. Please supervise your children when using devices.
