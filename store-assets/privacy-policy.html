Privacy Policy for Screen Lock for Kids

Last updated: June 28, 2025

Overview

Screen Lock for Kids is designed to help parents temporarily disable touch interaction on their Android device while their child watches videos.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not:

  • Access the internet
  • Collect or store personal information
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Share data with third parties
  • Access device contacts, location, or files

Permissions Used

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display the floating lock button over other apps
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To keep the floating button visible

Data Storage

Any PIN you set is stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted shared preferences. We cannot access this data.

Contact

For any privacy questions, please contact the developer.
